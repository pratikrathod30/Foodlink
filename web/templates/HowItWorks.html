How FoodLink Works

A transparent, approval-based system that ensures food reaches the right hands without confusion.

01

Donor Lists Food

Donors add surplus food by providing quantity, pickup location and availability time.

Status: Available
02

Receivers Request Food

Receivers view all available food listings and send requests by submitting their pickup location.

Status: Requested
03

Donor Approves One

Multiple requests can be received. The donor reviews them and approves only one receiver.

Status: Approved
04

Food Collected

The approved receiver collects the food. The donor then marks the listing as collected.

Status: Collected

What if food is not collected?

If a receiver cancels or does not collect the food, the listing becomes available again for other receivers. Successfully collected food appears in receiver history.
